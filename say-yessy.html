Venues...the perfect place to Say-Yessy

Elmore Court, Gloucestershire £14,500 to £17,000
Exclusive use of this Grade II listed building and gardens, with external Gillyflower reception rooms for evening celebrations. Includes rehearsal dinner, overnight stay and Wedding Brunch for all guests. Talented catering staff are available for an additional cost per head.


Tipi Events, Chew Valley Lake £8,500 to £10,000
Three day hire of an idyllic spot overlooking the Lake, complete with multiple Tipis, delicious catering and trained/licenced bar staff. Further decorations, fire pit, and additional camping fields for overnight guests are optional.


Clearwell Castle, Coleford £5,500 to £7,500
Voted "Best Castle Wedding" in the 2017 UK Awards. Clearwell boasts an all-inclusive wedding day package, along with the option to select a Personal Wedding Manager dedicated to organising your celebrations, from ceremony to evening reception.


The Boathouse, Bath £3,500 to £4,000
This small venue overlooking the river Avon is perfect for a Spring/Summer wedding, boasting beautiful wildlife and the potential to hire a quick cruise for guests!


The Guildhall, Bath £90 to £440
Depending on the day of the week and number of guests, there's a choice of three rooms: Abbey View, Russell or Alkmaar. You can also choose a "statutory" option with two witnesses. The guildhall is a small and personal venue just across the road from the Abbey.
